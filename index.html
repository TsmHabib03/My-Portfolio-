My Journey

Hi, I’m Habib, a Grade 12 student with a strong interest in software development. Ever since I was a child, I have been fascinated by technology, especially how cell phone components and processors work. I also have a deep interest in Artificial Intelligence and its potential to transform the world. Using AI has greatly helped me as a programmer, making it easier to write thousands of lines of code efficiently. However, I also recognize its downsides, such as the risk of becoming overly reliant on it and not fully understanding the logic behind solutions.

I believe in the power of clean code, innovative thinking, and continuous learning. Every project I work on is an opportunity to grow and contribute to the tech community, while learning to balance the benefits and limitations of AI in my development journey.
